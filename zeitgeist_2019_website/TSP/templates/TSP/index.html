About TSP

Zeitgeist 2021 is proud to announce its second edition of School Outreach Program: Techno School Program(TSP). It will be a two-phase competition with talented students from prestigious schools participating in two categories namely:
Category 1: Class IX & X
Category 2: Class XI & XII
 The events organized for school students are as follows:
1. Zeitgeist Open Olympiad:

  • Preliminary test on 18 Jan 2020 (Saturday) in respective schools
  • The finale on Feb 2020(Saturday) at IIT Ropar campus
  • The prizes of the competition are worth 25k.
  • Top 25 participants from each category will be selected for the finale.
  • Registration fee for the Olympiad is INR 50
  • Please register your schools at www.advitiya/TSP.in

2. Techwalk

On the days of Zeitgeist 2020, all the participating labs in our institute will be open to the school students. Various departments will be showcasing various cutting-edge technologies in their particular field. Apart from the labs, there will be an exhibition where projects made by students, faculties will be displayed. There will be exhibitors from across the world who will display the emerging technologies. For the visitors, this will be a great chance to learn, experience and interact with experts from different scientific fields and also to witness the recent advancements in the field of science.

3. Junior Scientist Competition:
  • The finale on 8th Feb 2020(Saturday) at IIT Ropar campus
  • Each school is allowed to bring three projects accompanied by three students each .
  • Students should bring a brief printed report along with their project.
  • There is no registration fee for this competition .
