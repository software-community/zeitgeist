Zenith

Case study : (Max. people in team - 3) In this event Problem statement about astronomy will be declared 1 or 2 weeks before the event, and participants have to come up with its solution. You will understand the details once the problem statement reaches you.

Astrophotography : (only one person, no teams) Participants have to submit the photographs of celestial objects which they have taken from their camera or telescope which will be judged and winners will be declared by the club. Note that Plagiarism will not be tolerated in this event and you will be disqualified from the event.

Astroquiz : (Team size max. 3) Quiz on the topic of astronomy will be conducted in 2 phases, once a team qualifies in the first round they will be shifted to the second round for further shortlisting.

Aeromodelling

Aero Deli: The objective of this competition is to design an UAV integrated with a cooling system that can be used to deliver covid vaccines to the remote areas of our country.

Aero Quiz: If you like reading newspapers and being updated with the latest news all around the world in aircrafts & its recent technologies,etc then this Quiz would bring you a lot of fun and surprises for you.


Coding & Softcom

DebugIt: Chef is poor at coding. He has submitted some codes for his ACM ICPC online round, but he thinks that the code has some bugs. Help Chef find test cases where his code will fail, so that he can alter it before submitting and fulfill his dream of winning ICPC for India.

P=NP: Competitive Programming based contest where teams would be given some problem statements and would have to submit codes in order to get AC.

Age Limit Exceeded: Students would be given a problem statement for which they will have to submit code and get an AC. Any school going student can participate in the event. Valid school id must be presented to receive the prize.

Hackathon Use artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies to develop innovative solutions for modern problems. Present your solution to a panel of judges.


Fincom

Case Study competition: In this competition your interpretation, analytical skills and case solving skills will be required to create the best solution for the given problem statement. The competition will consist of three rounds with guesstimates round, case study solving round and online presentation of the case study ppt followed by the Q&A.

Stock stalkers: A virtual portfolio management competition which will be conducted on a dedicated stock simulation platform on the live market for 1 week. The aim is to maximize the value of the portfolio in the given time interval.

Business Quiz :This competition will test your knowledge on finance , business, etc .


CIM

INVENTO: A fortnight-long design challenge which will test your team’s (2-5 members) innovation to tackle real world challenges in the most efficient way.

SpeeDAC: Think you’re the fastest 3D modeller? Show your skills in 2 hours of gruelling time limited modelling challenge where every second counts, literally! 2D drawings of parts will be given on spot. Event will be proctored in a video conference.

E-Cell

An event focussing on developing the attitude of Problem Solving. There are hundreds of unsolved problems, yet we can't see the gap! Those who solve them become great Entrepreneurs. The participants/Teams will get a fixed set of Information, and they have to develop a Solution and Pitch it.


Mechanical Department

Mechanical Madness: This event is based on Rube Goldberg Machines: Complicated, deliberately overengineered contraptions that ultimately perform a very simple task. Ironically, they’re rarely (if ever) built by rubes. One step triggers the next in a chain reaction until the final task is complete.

Weapon Star: suppose you are in a war,and you need to fight back to the enemy for survival. Obviously you'll need a gun to fight a modern war. Here's a challenge for you. Can you create a 2d model gun with limited sources?
