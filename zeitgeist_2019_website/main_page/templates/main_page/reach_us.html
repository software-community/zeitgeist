How to Reach:

1. Arrive Chandigarh (well connected through air and land transport).

2. By Train

2.1 Board a train to Rupnagar Railway Station (Station Code - RPAR).

2.2 Book an auto to IIT ROPAR "Main Campus" (will cost about Rs. 120, but you can try bargaining).

2.3 Reach IIT ROPAR Transit Campus TC-1 (walking distance from Rupnagar Railway Station). Busses at regular intervals are arranged from that campus to the Main campus (free of cost).

3. By Bus

3.1 Take a bus to Rupnagar from Chandigarh (ISBT Sector 43).

3.2 Board off from the bus at New Bus Stand, Rupnagar, and follow 2.1.

3.3 Board off from the bus at "Purana (old) Bus Stand" and follow 2.1 or 2.2.
