About Us

Zeitgeist is one of the biggest annual cultural festival of North India: A fiesta where art, talent, and diversity meets. It has a total footfall of about 12,000 and a reach of more than 250 colleges. Encapsulating the spirit of times, Zeitgeist provides an opportunity to the people from all over India to showcase their talent. It provides a platform to the young talents of our nation to stir the atmosphere, rock the audience and set the stage on fire. Zeitgeist has hosted many talented and celebrated personalities like Vishal-Shekhar, Mohit Chauhan, Alfaz, Zephyrtone and DJ Olly Esse. Filled with the exhilaration of heated competition and exuberant events, Zeitgeist 2k19 is here to rock your heart.

Responsibilities

    • Ensure maximum registration and participation from your college.

  • Publicise Zeitgeist :

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Campus Ambassador?

    • Campus Ambassadors are active leaders in their college who act as representatives of Zeitgeist.

  • How to apply for Campus Ambassador?

    • By clicking on 'Register' button at the top of this page.

  • What is the eligibility to become a Campus Ambassador?

    • One must own valid ID card issued in his/her name from the college whose Campus Ambassador he/she wants to be.

  • Can there be more than one Campus Ambassador from the same college?

    • If student participation is relatively high from a college, then more than one Campus Ambassadors may be appointed.
